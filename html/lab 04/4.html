From Garden to Plate

A potager is a French term for an ornamentel vegetable or kitchen garden. Often flowers (edible and non-edible) and herbs planted with the vegetables to enhance the garden's beauty. The goal is to make the function of providing food aesthetically pleasing.


What to Plant

Plants are chosen as much for their functionality as for their color and form. Many are trained to grow upward. A well-design potager can provide food, cut flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintenance. Potagers can disguise their function of providing for a home in a wide array of forms- from the caretree style of the cottage garden to the formatily of a knot garden.
